Penne Alla Vodka

Description

Don't be discouraged by vodka in the recipe ingredients, it doesn't differ much from the addition of wine in many Italian recipes. The sauce is delicious, creamy - tomato-based with the addition of cream, and vodka adds a slight spiciness to the whole dish.

Ingredients

  • 300g penne pasta
  • 400g canned peeled tomatoes
  • 1 shallot
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 150g 30% cream
  • 80g vodka
  • 50g butter
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Basil

What to do

First, saut√© finely chopped shallot in a pan. Then add crushed garlic cloves. Once the onion becomes translucent, add the tomatoes. Evaporate most of the water from the tomatoes. Next, add the cream, vodka, and a sprig of basil. Reduce the sauce over low heat, ensuring the vodka evaporates. When the sauce thickens, turn off the heat, remove the basil sprig, and add butter and Parmesan cheese to taste. Mix the sauce with pasta cooked AL DENTE (!!). Serve with parsley and freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
